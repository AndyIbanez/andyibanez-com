This website is pretty simple, and so is this privacy policy. Here's what you should know:

1. **Hosting**: This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log your IP address or other metadata when you visit. Please refer to [GitHub’s Privacy Policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement) for details.

2. **Analytics**: I use Google Analytics to understand how people find and use my content. I don’t care about tracking individuals, and I don’t attempt to personally identify anyone. I mostly look at page views, referral sources, and traffic trends.

3. **Static Site**: This site is built with [Hugo](https://gohugo.io), a static site generator. There’s no dynamic backend or database. To the best of my knowledge, no cookies are being set by me.

4. **Cloudflare**: Cloudflare is used for DNS and basic analytics. Like with Google, I use it to get a second opinion on site traffic — not to track you.

5. **TL;DR**: I don’t know who you are, and I don’t want to. But I’m very interested in what websites sent you here.
